My Work

In 2007 life took over and a series of unfortunate events happened next. I quit professional dancing and started traveling solo, in search of my soul's purpose. For some years I lived outside society, where I met the nicest and weirdest people, lived off grid, discovered coconuts and vegan eating, and found my next true passion: Tribal Fusion bellydance. Whenever I could I took up workshops with, what I thought to be, the best dancers in the bellydance world. Below you'll find my resume and a list of my teachers.
My happiest memories are of traveling France and Thailand solo, meeting up with random musicians to perform together. Yoga came to bless me along my path and it was a happy time of recalibration and these years away shaped me as a person and a teacher. The dreams of starting back up again never left and one day I found myself doing just that. Bellydance and yoga changed my life, and everyday I am grateful that I get to do what I am most passionate about. It's a dream come true. And if you happen to read this, I am happy to meet you, you're awesome, and we should dance together!
