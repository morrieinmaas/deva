Goddess Studio

The studio is located at a quiet location in the middle of town, in an beautiful old theatre-building known as 'huis van bourgondie'. The artistic atmosphere is still present. The studio is equipped with a mirror and light coloured wooden flooring. With its high ceiling and big spacious window, there is plenty of daylight, creating a very pleasant feel to the room. The room is heated and warm. Together, all these elements create a cosy environment suitable for teaching in an intimate setting.

Maximum number of students:

  • Yoga: 10
  • Bellydance: 7


Getting there

The studio is close to the centre and easy accessible with free parking spaces inside the terrain and a bike rack. Unfortunately by government's decreed the studio is not freely accessible. The gates are open 15 minutes before class and close 2 min before start. So please be on time!
