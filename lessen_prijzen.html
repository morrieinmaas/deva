    RaqsFusion lvl 2

    Raqs level 1 This level is for those who are new to bellydance or wish to polish up their skills. In our Raqs level 1 level you will build a strong foundation and intensive knowledge of the bellydance repertoire. You will learn the basics of hip- and chest isolations, snake arms, travelings steps and become a queen
    of the shimmies! Next these techniques are added together in a fun and playful combinations. We'll be working towards
    a fun easy to learn choreography that allows you to easily keep track of the progress you make.

    New Raqsfusion lvl 1: 17 March 2016 !!

    You have a reasonable foundation in bellydance and know the general basic bellydance repertoire. Our Raqs level 2
    is exactly what you need for finetuning your skills. This level has a stronger focus on refinement and you'll start working on layering. Meaning you will bring your isolations to the next level. Here your dance will become alive. Classes are based on the RaqsFusion 1 curriculum with an extensive focus on fusion. We'll be adding techniques from Burlesque, Tribal, Temple dance and Yoga.

    Currently we have an ongoing weekly intermediate level.
