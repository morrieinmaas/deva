style of bellydance



Our style of bellydance has its roots in classical oriental dance ( Raqs Sharki) Departing from Raqs Sharki, you'll find elements of other styles of movement such as yoga, modern, ballet, hiphop, burlesque, flamenco, gypsy, hindi, tempel and tribal fusion bellydance. It's a very modern way of bellydancing with a lot of respect to the original cultures. We dance to various styles of music, from traditional to arabic pop and tribal fusion.



set up of classes



Classes are attuned to the level of the participants, but you're also challenged to kick your but. Classes are mixed with humor and have a beginner friendly way of breaking teachniques into comprehensive pieces. All classes follow the same set up of warm up, repetition last class, technique and or choreo and cool down. Just as in yoga we correct your posture and help you get it right. We reserve plenty of time for this. Depending on the course you're partaking in, classes last from 1 hour to 1, 5 hours.



different levels



In total there are 3 levels of bellydance. We can further subdivide each level into 2 levels. Therefor you'll find different levels in one class. Each level consist of approximate 20 classes.

  • Beginner/ beginner+
  • Intermediate/intermediate +
  • Advanced/ advanced+



When your group advances you will automatically shift to the next level.
