Welcome at DevaDivine!

What better way to feel good in your skin than by dancing? And in particular bellydance?! This way of moving has been a blessing for so many girls and women. You get to have a nice workout, burn calories, work on your abs, thighs and buns, and it's so much fun!

More

Hi and welcome to my website. My name is Laura. I am the creative mind behind Deva Divine.
We specialize in multiple styles of yoga and bellydance. My artist name is also Deva Divine.

Bellydance is a thing that seemed to have just happened to me. In my early twenties a friend brought
me along to dance class, we started straight away at intermediate level. My first impression was that it was really challenging. But next thing I knew I got absoultely hooked, from that moment on, my life was all about bellydance. I loved it so much because it was the first time that I took pride in how my body moved, it made me feel sexy and confident. So, within a year my formar teacher introduced me to a professional bellydance education where I spent the next two years learning how to be a profestional performer and teacher. During this period I set up my own bellydance school called Bella Esme and dragged my cd player and pile of cd's across the country performing/ teaching and giving workshops.
I took great pride in seeing young girls blossom into confident ladies. We had a lot of fun!

Who I Am

In 2007 life took over and a series of unfortunate events happened next. I quit professional dancing and started traveling solo, in search of my soul's purpose. For some years I lived outside society, where I met the nicest and weirdest people, lived off grid, discovered coconuts and vegan eating, and found my next true passion: Tribal Fusion bellydance. Whenever I could I took up workshops with, what I thought to be, the best dancers in the bellydance world. Below you'll find my resume and a list of my teachers.

My Inspiration

My happiest memories are of traveling France and Thailand solo, meeting up with random musicians
to perform together. Yoga came to bless me along my path and it was a happy time of recalibration
and these years away shaped me as a person and a teacher. The dreams of starting back up again
never left and one day I found myself doing just that. Bellydance and yoga changed my life, and everyday I am grateful that I get to do what I am most passionate about.
It's a dream come true. And if you happen to read this, I am happy to meet you, you're awesome, and we should dance together!
