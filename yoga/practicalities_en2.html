Practical Info

More

Studio Deva Divine is an alternative studio that specializes in yoga and bellydance. Since 2014 we've been teaching at our wonderful studio down town Maastricht, in a beautiful old theatre building. We love to listen to our students and they enjoy spending their time with us, as you can read in some of the loving reviews. We often take courses, for example on yogatherapy and alignment, and we're never done studying, ensures a safe environment for our students in which they can practice yoga freely.

Yoga Classes

In yoga we combine breath and movement. If you are new to yoga, you will notice that there is a lot to pay attention too. For example: a right posture, focus and attention, muscle tension and breathing. For a beginner it can be quite a lot, but you will see, given some time, it will come naturally quite fast. The body is great at adapting to yoga. As a beginning yogi you will be advised to pay more attention to the proper execution of the postures. The breath will follow suit. Classes all have a similar 'set up', which you'll get to perceive its structure usually within 2-3 classes. Then the real yoga will kick off, you will become part of the flow that arises in class, with your breathe to guide you. The studio works with physical cues, meaning the teacher will guide you deeper within the posture, to ensure a good aligment, which allows the body to perform optimally.

How we roll


Deva Divine is the only studio that works without a contract. Motivation is something of an intrinsic nature and we feel that it cannot be forced by some contract. Because of the voluntariness of it all, it creates a very relaxed atmosphere, where people are motivated and happy to come back to us. With our no-bullshit system, it's easy to come and go. But what we see is the tendency that people actually stay with us longer. There is a lot of tolerance and mutual interest between our friendly students. And most important: there is always more compassion than competition.

We sell cards of 5 or 10 visits and work with 'try outs and drop ins'. Check our pricing table for our tarifs and/ or privacy policy for more information. Studio location



The studio is located in an old theatre building at Hoogfrankrijk 27 (From Brandweer go up the cobble street, from Kumulus take the road down. We are in the heart of Maastricht. Halfway the street "Hoogfrankrijk"- next to the car exit of the Beyaard- you'll find a big green gate. Use the entrance door on its left. From here you'll access the parking lot. You find the studio within the building on your left. Follow the signs (very easy to find)! The gate usually opens 15 min in advance and closes 1 min before class. Please make sure to arrive on time! If you know you're going be late, notify via phone or Whatsapp so we can make arrangements for you to enter and not have to miss your class.

Safety



If you have any injury or ailment worth notifying, please let us know, it's important to us! This way we can give you related options to make sure you have a safe and nice practice.

We count on your own council to be able to work within the boundaries and limitations of your body. Working with breath and body-consciousness lowers the chance of injury dramatically, that Vinyasa yoga is said to be one of the safest yoga practices.
