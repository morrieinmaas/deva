    Classes, prices and discounts

    1: If you're new to the studio you become our friend and get a free ticket, how this works is explained below.

    2: If you're not, your next option is to get your try out reimbursed if you sign up for one of our courses.

    3: By subscribing yourself to the newsletter and 'like' our facebook page, you become a friend of the studio.

    (This service is only available for those that are new and unregistered with the studio!)

    Currently it works like this: send us an email with your facebook name to verify, and you will receive a ticket that allows you to make reservations for your free class. If the studio does not get a reservation you will have to pay the regular price for your class. When you bring your ticket the next time, you can join for free, or get reimbursed. This counts for both bellydance and yoga.
