Vinyasa poweryoga for beginners

Practical

During yoga we combine movement and breath to unite mind and body. If you are new to yoga there is a lot going on at once, the cues might seem a bit overwhelming to follow up on. This is completely normal. Beginner students are advised to focus on following the movements, breath will follow naturally within a few classes. Classes have a similar set-up. Usually within 2-3 classes you will start to perceive the structure of the class. From there on things only get easier and more exciting!

There might be physical corrections, not because you are doing anything wrong, but to guide you to a more safe and stable posture if not aligned properly. Please notify me in advance if you prefer not to be touched (which is totally okay).

Donation&registration

The class is donation based (estimate is around 3-5€) depending on your wallet and how you like the class. It is registration-fee free and you can drop in. There is limited space reserved for max 10 yogi's. So, it is important to notify me before 12AM the day you want to attend class, that you are coming. Let me know if you bring friends too. You can notify me through mail or WhatsApp: 06-24424270. Or through Facebook, add me if you wish (Devi Kali). My page is called yoga&fusion style bellydance, there you will find updates on lessons, events and other yoga-related issues (you must first like the page to be able to receive updates).

Clothes

Loose comfy or tight flexible clothing is advised (make sure leggings are not see-through or to baggy that they come off when moving :)). It is recommended to wear a tight T-shirt/singlet, so your attention can remain on the practice and not so much on tucking in T-shirts. You can bring a meditation-pillow or a small light blanket for during the relaxation-phase. If you have a yogamat, please bring it! Otherwise there are some spares to use. From hygienic viewpoint it is often advised to bring your own.

Location

The studio is located within an old theatre building at, Hoogfrankrijk 27, centre of Maastricht, one street behind Lumiere.
The big green gate opens 15 minutes in advance and will close one minute before starting time. You have to be on time, otherwise there's no access to the building. We're sorry for the latecomers.

Injuries&ailments

Let us know if you have any injuries or any other ailments. The studio cannot take any reliability for injuries during class. We rely on your own wisdom and knowing your body. Working together with the breath and body awareness will greatly reduce any risk, therefore yoga is considered one of the safest practices around.


If any more questions, please don't hesitate to ask. Use the contact form, text or call me.
