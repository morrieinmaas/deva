Studio

Studio Deva Divine is an alternative studio that specializes in yoga and belly dance. Since 2014 we've been teaching at our wonderful studio down town Maastricht, in a beautiful old theatre building. We love to listen to our students and they enjoy spending their time with us, as you can read in some of the loving reviews. We often take courses, for example on yoga therapy and alignment, and we're never done studying whilst ensuring a safe environment for our students in which they can practice yoga freely.

About Deva

Belly dance is a thing that seemed to have just happened to me. In my early twenties a friend brought me along to dance class, we started straight away at intermediate level. My first impression was that it was really challenging. But next thing I knew I got absolutely hooked, from that moment on, my life was all about belly dance. I loved it so much because it was the first time that I took pride in how my body moved, it made me feel sexy and confident.

So, within a year my formar teacher introduced me to a professional belly dance education where I spent the next two years learning how to be a profestional performer and teacher. During this period I set up my own belly dance school called Bella Esme and dragged my CD player and pile of CD's across the country performing/ teaching and giving workshops I took great pride in seeing young girls blossom into confident ladies. We had a lot of fun!

My Work

In 2007 life took over and a series of unfortunate events happened next. I quit professional dancing and started traveling solo, in search of my soul's purpose. For some years I lived outside society, where I met the nicest and weirdest people, lived off grid, discovered coconuts and vegan eating, and found my next true passion: Tribal Fusion Belly Dance. Whenever I could I took up workshops with, what I thought to be, the best dancers in the belly dance world. Below you'll find my resume and a list of my teachers.

My happiest memories are of traveling France and Thailand solo, meeting up with random musicians to perform together. Yoga came to bless me along my path and it was a happy time of recalibration and these years away shaped me as a person and a teacher. The dreams of starting back up again never left and one day I found myself doing just that.

Belly dance and yoga changed my life, and everyday I am grateful that I get to do what I am most passionate about. It's a dream come true. And if you happen to read this, I am happy to meet you, you're awesome, and we should dance together!
