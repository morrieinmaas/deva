Our belly dance style

Our style of belly dance has its roots in classical oriental dance ( Raqs Sharki) Departing from Raqs Sharki, you'll find elements of other styles of movement such as yoga, modern, ballet, hiphop, burlesque, flamenco, gypsy, hindi, tempel and tribal fusion belly dance. It's a very modern way of bellydancing with a lot of respect to the original cultures. We dance to various styles of music, from traditional to arabic pop and tribal fusion.

Set up of classes

Classes are attuned to the level of the participants, but you're also challenged to kick your but. Classes are mixed with humor and have a beginner friendly way of breaking techniques into comprehensive pieces. All classes follow the same set up: warm up, repetition of the last class, learning new techniques and/or choreo followed by cooling down. Just as in yoga we correct your posture and help you to get it right. We reserve plenty of time for this. Depending on the course you're partaking in, classes last from 1 hour to 1,5 hours.

Different levels

In total there are 3 levels of belly dance. We can further subdivide each level into 2 levels.
Therefore you'll find different levels in one class. Each level consist of approximately 20 classes.

  • Beginner/ beginner+
  • Intermediate/intermediate +
  • Advanced/ advanced+

When your group advances you will automatically shift to the next level.
